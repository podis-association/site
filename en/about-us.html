About Us

Home > About Us

REGIONAL ASSOCIATION OF FARMERS AND ENTERPRISES "PODIȘ" NGO is a non-profit, apolitical, useful and civil interest association from Dumbrăvița commune, Singerei district, Republic of Moldova.

It represents the visions and interests of its members, pursuing the development of multi-partnerships with compatible (Romanian) and foreign communities that promote local advocacy and lobbying policies in favor of implementing projects or other public, community, regional and extra-regional utility programs.

It is dedicated to economic and non-economic activities, credit and investment, attracting investments and grants, multiplying relevant partnerships and cooperation, social and public support, with non-profit benefits and common interest for Romanian communities. from everywhere.
