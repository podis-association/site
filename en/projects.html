Projects

Home > Projects

2012/2013 - Elaboration and promotion of the construction project of the biomass- based heating station for the heating of the Gymnasium and Kindergarten in Dumbrăvița village financed by USAID (2.5 million lei MDL)

2014 - Elaboration and promotion of the road construction project in white version for the group - customer beneficiary of producers (AO PODIȘ) co-financed by IFAD with 85% (2.8 million lei MDL)

2015/2016 - Elaboration and promotion of the repair and thermal insulation project of the gymnasium building (60 thousand lei MDL) and of the kindergarten (940 thousand lei MDL) in partnership with Dumbrăvița City Hall, projects co-financed by the Energy Efficiency Fund of Moldova

2016 - Facilitation and implementation in partnership with Dumbrăvița City Hall of the project on the construction of the sanitary block at the gymnasium in Dumbrăvița village financed by Eco-San (1.0 million lei MDL)

2017 - Elaboration and promotion of the project of interior capital repairs of the kindergarten building in Dumbrăvița village co-financed by the Social Investment Fund of Moldova (800 thousand lei MDL)

2020 - Obtaining charitable assistance and funding through Philanthropy Contract allocated by BATICOM UNIP LDA Portugal (EUR 10285) for the development and promotion of the concept of the agro-tourism and production platform with beneficial regional and international impact dedicated to local, regional and foreign visitors interested in knowledge of the traditions, hospitality and leisure culture of the Republic of Moldova.
